# K-12 Phishing Defense

## Objective
Turn campaign and email security data into concrete risk-reduction actions.

## Workflow
1. ingest phishing campaign and user outcome data.
2. identify high-risk departments and repeat click patterns.
3. validate SPF/DKIM/DMARC posture and enforcement gaps.
4. map findings to awareness and technical control actions.
5. produce leadership-ready trend summary.

## Guardrails
- avoid blaming users, focus on system and training improvement.
- ensure executive metrics are reproducible from source data.
- tag credential exposure findings for priority response.

## Required Artifacts
- phishing risk trend summary.
- control gap list for email authentication posture.
- prioritized training follow-up actions.
