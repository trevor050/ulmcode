Normalize supervised command outputs into durable evidence records and unverified leads.

Use this after `command_supervise` writes raw output, after resume when command
artifacts exist but evidence is sparse, and before finding validation/reporting.

The tool reads explicit artifact paths or discovered `command-plan.json` files,
parses structured outputs when possible, writes `evidence-index.json`,
`leads.json`, and citable `evidence_record` entries. Leads are intentionally
not findings; promote a lead with `finding_record` only after validation.
